What is DebateWatch?

What We Do?

DebateWatch is a website designed to make national tournament records for high school debaters more accessible. Created by a few students at Bridgewater-Raritan High School, the website was born out of what we felt was an essential need for the debate community that had not yet been filled. The backend is programmed in Python, featuring DebateWatchâ€™s proprietary algorithm, which enables us to scrape locally stored tournament data and bring it to users' fingertips within seconds. The algorithm itself efficiently analyzes tabroom.com, a website that stores raw data for debaters. It is able to output all necessary information about a singular debater or partnership, acting as a hub of historical records.

History

In just a few months since our release last year, DebateWatch welcomed was visited by over 20,000 visitors in just one month. Throughout the course of the debate season, the website recieved over 80,000 hits.
